What data do we collect?

We collect and process data when you register for an account. Information you provide may include: email address and social media usernames. If you choose to signup using Google or GitHub we will not collect any extra personal identification information.

How will we use your data?

This site was created as an educational project and we won't ever email you or share your data with anyone else.

How do we store your data?

Your data is stored on a Heroku server in the European Union.

Your data protection rights

You can permanently delete your account and all associated information at any time by visiting your profile, clicking Edit Profile, then Delete Account.

Cookies

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology

For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org.

How do we use cookies?

If you register, we use cookies in to improve your experience by keeping you signed in unless you manually sign out.

We don't collect information about your browser, device or IP address, and we don't use cookies for advertising.

The above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser or set your browser not to accept cookies. Site functionality may not function as expected without cookies.
